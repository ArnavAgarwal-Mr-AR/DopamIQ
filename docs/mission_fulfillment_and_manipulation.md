# Mission Fulfillment: Unmasking the Platform Shadow

## Core Objective
The primary mission of this project is to explicitly reveal **"What Netflix knows about us from our data."** This document outlines how the architectural layers of the Behavioral Scoring Engine bridge the gap between raw CSV files and deep psychological profiling.

## 1. Fulfillment of the Mission
Netflix's competitive advantage is not its content library, but its mathematical understanding of human behavior. This project reverse-engineers that advantage using the following pillars:

*   **Behavioral Attribution**: We convert innocent timestamps into aggressive personality traits. A "Watch" event at 1 AM isn't just a record; it's a data point for `Biological Vulnerability`.
*   **Predictive Awareness**: By establishing a `Binge Probability` and `Expected Duration`, we demonstrate that the platform knows you are going to watch 4 more episodes before you've even finished the first.
*   **The Temporal Footprint**: Through the `Trends` and `Heatmap` logic, we expose that the platform has mapped your weekly lifecycle—it knows exactly when your willpower is depleted.

## 2. The Manipulation Report (New Feature)
To crown the mission, we have implemented the **Manipulation Intelligence Report**. This section directly evaluates how the platform's UI "hooks" successfully hijack user autonomy.

### Key Metrics Tracked:
| Metric | Description | Mathematical Proxy |
|--------|-------------|--------------------|
| **Autoplay Hijack Rate** | How often the algorithm successfully chose your next session without your manual intervention. | `autoplay_ratio` |
| **Loop Retention** | The probability that once you engage with the platform, you will fall into a 3+ episode loop. | `binge_factor` |
| **Willpower Fatigue** | The percentage of your viewing that occurs during "Vulnerable Windows" (23:00 - 03:00). | `late_night_ratio` |
| **Algorithm Dependency** | Whether you find content through the search engine or rely entirely on recommendations. | `abandonment_rate` & `search_activity` |

### Intelligence Summary
The report provides a high-level **Threat Assessment**. It identifies precisely which behavioral channel (passive consumption vs. biological windows) is the platform's primary entry point for attention capture.

## 3. Conclusion
This tool transforms the user from a **Passive Consumer** being analyzed by an algorithm into an **Informed Subject** who can see the algorithm's reflection. By surfacing these "Manipulation Metrics," we fulfill the core objective of revealing the invisible psychological strings that govern the viewing experience.
